About Us

Learn More About Us

Djococo is a leading charcoal product supplier in Indonesia. We are a company engaged in manufacturing such as high quality superior charcoal products. We manage these coconut derivatives almost all over Indonesia, especially on the island of Java. proper production and maximum drying and we make sure the briquettes we make have no cracks, there are many benefits for example:

  • Free from peculiar smell and taste
  • No cracks and sparks
  • Thin ash content and light ash color
  • Long burning time and constant heat
  • No fire, no odor and no smoke
  • Customized order is allowed as per your requirement

Djococo is here to contribute to reducing dependence on fuel oil and gas, and support the utilization of coconut shells instead. Therefore Djococo provides quality coconut shell briquette products from Indonesia.

Why Choose Djococo?

We present an environmentally friendly energy innovation with our coconut shell briquette product from Djo Coco. We provide the highest quality coconut shell briquettes on the market. Join us in realizing an eco-friendly lifestyle by using coconut shell briquettes from Djo Coco.
